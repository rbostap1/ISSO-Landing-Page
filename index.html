How to Join

  1. Complete the Anti-Hazing Training

    By state law and university policy, you must complete the required Anti-Hazing training before joining any student organization.

  2. Join Our Discord Server

    Click the Discord link to join our community. This is where we share real-time updates, meeting times, and any important information.

  3. Request Membership

    Head to KSU Engage and sign in. Search for ISSO and fill out the "Membership Request Form". Application deadline is TBD for Spring 2026. Applications open on January 5th, 2026.

  4. Get Involved

    Participate in events, volunteer for roles, and connect with members. There are many ways to contribute and grow with us.
